About Us

Who are we?

We are the summer camp focused on a different social program every day. Our weekly planning makes our center a creator of new experiences.

For Whom?

Our camp is for kids from 8 to 16 years old who love creating and having new adventures every day.

Our Goals

Our goal is to teach the kids how to make a difference even with a small act of kindness while making fun activities to strengthen values ​​and make a better world

Programs

Senior's Day

A visit to a nursing home where they can share with the elderly listening to their experiences or simply doing activities together.

Protector's Day

The environment is our main concern of the day. Recycling, planting trees, or simply creating art with recycled materials.

Animal's Day

Spending time with animals who live in a shelter will make them realize that pets aren't toys, and their caretakers will teach them the benefits of having an adopted animal.

Cooking's Day

Kids will cook a nutritious meal and serve it to people without housing. Working together will teach them about teamwork and how grateful should they be.

Sport's Day

Our last day is to team up with kids from an orphanage and have fun playing different sports and getting the chance to win the "Kindness Cup."
